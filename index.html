About JuniorCoders

Pioneering STEM education in Zimbabwe and across Africa

Our Mission

JuniorCoders is dedicated to bridging the digital divide in Africa by providing world-class robotics and programming education to young learners. We believe that every child deserves access to cutting-edge technology education, regardless of their background.

Our Approach

We start with visual programming using Scratch and Blockly to make coding accessible and fun, then gradually progress to text-based programming languages and web development. Our curriculum includes exciting game development using Godot engine, ensuring students stay engaged while learning essential programming concepts through hands-on projects.
